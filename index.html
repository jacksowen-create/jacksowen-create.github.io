A Reliable, Scalable Distribution Partner

JG Distributions provides brand owners, manufacturers, and logistics partners with a dependable channel into UK and EU consumer marketplaces. We manage the full operational cycle—from procurement and inventory management through to last-mile delivery—allowing our supply partners to extend market reach without operational complexity.

Leveraging strategic cross-border logistics and localized European fulfillment networks to deliver premium brands directly to digital marketplaces. Our infrastructure is designed for speed, compliance, and scalability across both UK and EU regulatory environments.

Our Core Retail & Distribution Sectors

We operate across three principal consumer goods categories, maintaining curated relationships with brand owners and procurement channels to deliver quality-assured product to digital marketplaces at scale.

Automotive & Precision Care

We source and retail a focused range of premium automotive care products—including specialist cleaning compounds, detailing solutions, and vehicle protection treatments—distributed through leading UK and EU digital marketplaces. Our category includes established specialist brands trusted across the professional and consumer detailing market.

Vehicle Care Detailing Solutions Premium Brands
Dermatological Skincare & Personal Care

Our personal care category spans skincare, haircare, and cosmetics—encompassing both dermatologically tested formulations and mass-premium consumer brands. We serve a broad consumer audience across the UK and European markets, supplying brand-name products through established multichannel retail channels.

Household Care & Consumer Cleaning Solutions

We distribute a commercially relevant range of household care and consumer cleaning products—including surface treatments, fabric care, and general household consumables.
